Identification Advisor An IGERT-ESP Adventure

Thank you for agreeing to participate in our survey. As part of an examination of teaching best practices we are interested in hearing about a past negative experience you may have had with a teacher. The responses you provide will be used to help identify possible areas of improvement in educational curriculum. Excerpts of the responses you provide might be used as examples in our final report.

All of your responses are voluntary and you can opt to cancel your participation in the survey at any time by leaving the web page.

If you are over the age of 18 and wish to continue please click "Begin Survey."

In an effort to generate initial content for our new site we would like for you to answer the following questions:

Please describe a negative experience you had with a teacher / professor.

When did this negative experience occur?

Please describe where this experience occured?

Please describe the context within which you had this experience. What led you and the teacher / professor to be in the situation in the first place?

Please provide a recommendation for how the problem could have been better resolved or corrected?
